Privacy Policy — Torogi

Last updated: October 23, 2025

  • Account Information — such as name, email address, and profile details when you register or log in.

  • Device Information — including device model, operating system, IP address, and diagnostics.
  • Ad Data — anonymized data used by Google AdMob to serve personalized or contextual ads.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Torogi app.
  • Enable communication and community interactions between users.

  • Display relevant ads through Google AdMob.
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security issues.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share limited data with:

  • Supabase — for authentication, database, and hosting.
  • Google AdMob — for ad personalization and delivery.
  • Authorities — if required by law or legal process.

4. Data Retention

We keep your information only as long as necessary to maintain your account, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. You can request deletion of your account and associated data anytime.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your data.
  • Opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.
  • Contact us directly with any privacy concerns.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure — but no system is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Torogi is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We’ll notify you of major updates via in-app notice or email.
